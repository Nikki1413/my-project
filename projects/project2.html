Coffee Making:

Ingredients

  • Gas Stove
  • Kettle
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Coffee Powder
  • Milk

Steps to Make a Simple Coffee

  1. First you Switch on the Stove
  2. Put a Kettle on the Stove
  3. Pour a water in kettle and heat about 1cup of water in a kettle
  4. Add Coffee Powder
  5. Add Sugar depending on your taste
  6. Stir well to mix the coffee, water, sugar until fully dissolved
  7. After some time add milk and stir for mild warm coffee
  8. After some more time your simple cup of coffee is ready!
